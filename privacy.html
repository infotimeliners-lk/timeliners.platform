Overview

TimeLiners (we, our, us) is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect data when you use our platform at timeliners.lk. By using TimeLiners, you agree to the practices described here.

Information We Collect

What you provide directly

  • Name, email address, and phone number during registration
  • Portfolio content including project files, videos, images, and descriptions
  • Payment details provided during subscription — handled securely by PayHere. We do not store card information.
  • Communications you send us via email

Collected automatically

  • IP address, browser type, and device information
  • Pages visited and features used on the platform
  • Analytics data to understand how the platform is used

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your TimeLiners account
  • To display your portfolio to visitors as intended
  • To process subscription payments and manage billing
  • To send account notifications such as plan renewals and expiry reminders
  • To provide customer support via email
  • To improve platform features based on usage patterns
  • To detect and prevent fraudulent or unauthorized activity

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except in these cases:

  • Payment processing: We use PayHere to process payments securely in compliance with PCI-DSS standards.
  • Infrastructure: We use Supabase for database and file storage, and Netlify for hosting. These providers handle data under their own security obligations.
  • Email delivery: We use Resend to send transactional emails on our behalf.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase with industry-standard encryption and Row Level Security policies ensuring users can only access their own data. While we take all reasonable measures, no system is entirely immune to security risks.

Your Portfolio & Public Content

Content you add to your public portfolio is visible to anyone who visits your portfolio link. You are responsible for ensuring you have the rights to content you upload. You can set individual projects to private from your dashboard at any time.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your data will be removed from our systems within 30 days, except where retention is required for legal or financial purposes.

Cookies

TimeLiners uses essential cookies to maintain your login session and platform functionality. We do not use third-party advertising cookies. Disabling cookies may affect platform functionality.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised date. Continued use of the platform after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
