Frequently Asked Questions

Everything about joining Timeliners, plans, and how profiles work.

How do I get a profile on Timeliners?
+
Choose a plan, tap the WhatsApp button and send your name, specialty, bio, and work samples. Our admin creates your profile within 24–48 hours.
Do I need to create an account myself?
+
No. Timeliners is fully managed. Message us on WhatsApp with your details — our team builds everything for you.
What is the difference between the three plans?
+
Starter (Free): Basic profile, up to 3 projects. Standard (1,990 LKR/mo): Unlimited projects, VIP badge, featured placement, analytics. VIP Pro (4,990 LKR/mo): Everything in Standard plus monthly social media promotion.
How does payment work?
+
All payments are manual. After messaging us on WhatsApp, we send bank account details. Transfer the fee, send confirmation, and your profile activates within 24 hours.
How do I post a job?
+
Message us on WhatsApp with the job details — role, type (full-time/freelance), location, and description. We'll post it on the Jobs page for free.
Can I update my portfolio after it's live?
+
Yes. Message us on WhatsApp with new work and we'll update your portfolio within 48 hours. Standard and VIP Pro get unlimited updates.
Still have questions?

We reply on WhatsApp within a few hours.
