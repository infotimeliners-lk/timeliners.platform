Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything about joining Timeliners, packages, and how profiles work.

How do I get a profile on Timeliners?
+
Choose a plan on the Join page, tap the WhatsApp button. Send us your name, specialty, bio, and work samples. Our admin creates your profile within 24–48 hours.
Do I need to create an account myself?
+
No. Timeliners is fully managed. Message us on WhatsApp with your details — our admin team builds everything for you.
What is the difference between the three plans?
+
Starter (Free): Basic profile, up to 3 portfolio items. Standard (1,990 LKR/mo): Unlimited portfolio, VIP badge, featured placement, analytics. VIP Pro (4,990 LKR/mo): Everything in Standard plus monthly social media promotion.
How does payment work?
+
All payments are manual. After messaging us on WhatsApp, we send bank account details. Transfer the fee, send the confirmation, and your profile activates within 24 hours. We accept bank transfers and WhatsApp Pay.
How does the like system work?
+
Visitors can like each creative's profile once per browser. Likes are stored so the same profile can't be liked twice from the same browser.
Can I update my portfolio after it's live?
+
Yes. Message us on WhatsApp with new work files and we'll update your portfolio within 48 hours. Standard and VIP Pro get unlimited updates.
Still have questions?

Our team replies on WhatsApp within a few hours.
